  • Mercury is the planet closest to the sun, the fastest moving, as well as the smallest planet. Because of the planet's proximity to the Sun, there is a huge range in temperature. Mercury is also heavily cratered, similar to Earth's moon. It has a very elliptical orbit and almost no atmosphere. The planet's name is derived from the Roman messenger god, Mercury. It was named this because they both share a trait: speed. People have been able to observe this planet since ancient because it can be seen without a teloscope. It has been visited by two spacecraft: Mariner 10 and MESSENGER.
