  • Mercury is the planet closest to the sun, as well as the smallest planet. Because of the planet's proximity to the Sun, there is a huge range in temperature. Mercury is also heavily cratered, similar to Earth's moon. It has a very elliptical orbit and almost no atmosphere. The planet's name is derived from the Roman messenger god, Mercury.
