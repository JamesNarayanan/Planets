  • Venus is the hottest, most toxic, and brightest planet. Because of its dense atmosphere made primarily of Carbon Dioxide, it holds heat the most of any planet, causing it to have the highest consistent surface temperature. It is also so bright at night that has been known as the Morning and Evening Star. Along with Uranus, Venus is the only planet to rotate clockwise about its axis. Venus also has the most circular orbit, only deviating from a circle by at most 1%. The planet's name is derived from the Roman god of love and beauty, Venus. It was given this name because of its beauty.
